THE LEDGER DECREE

Data transparency, sovereign tracking, and brotherhood accountability standards.

MANDATE: Caldera Commons is a functional arena built to record human discipline, labor output, and consistency. We believe your data belongs entirely to you. This decree outlines exactly how your actions are recorded and displayed.

1. The Extraction (What We Collect)

When you cross the threshold and authenticate with this system, we log:

  • Identity Markers: Your Google account email address and unique Google ID provider string.
  • Voluntary Records: The specific hours, actions, and biblical engine reflections you explicitly type and lock into the Task Hub.
  • Habit Completions: The timestamps of the daily routine chores you check off.

2. The Broadcast (How It Is Displayed)

This is a shared accountability ecosystem. Your logged actions do not sit in a corporate silo; they actively build the community platform:

  • Your parsed email name and target trade are listed openly on the public Guild Leaderboard alongside your total XP.
  • Your work logs and chore completions generate public notification cards on the Live Shift Feed for strategy discussion.

3. Absolute Safeguards (Our Covenant)

"Let your yes be yes, and your no, no."

We do not use tracking cookies, we do not deploy third-party analytics scripts, and we will never sell, barter, or commercialize a single syllable of your life's history to corporate advertisers. The data remains locked on our private database instance exclusively to serve your growth tracking.

4. Complete Erasure (The Kill Switch)

If you choose to leave the guild permanently, your records do not belong to us. At any time, you have the right to request a complete purge. We will manually incinerate your user profile and all connected shift logs from the database tables permanently.
