Thank you for checking out this project. This is a Christian application built from the trenches to serve the community, optimize resources, and keep our digital ecosystem open-source and free for everyone.

The Donationware Model

We do not charge for this software. In alignment with spiritual principles, we operate entirely on voluntary, free-will offerings. "God helps those who help themselves," and your support helps keep this infrastructure alive, sustainable, and scalable.

All donations go strictly toward Project Infrastructure Costs, including:

  • Maintaining and licensing our primary development hardware (Node 0 Desktop)
  • Future server tower, rack system, and micro-data center development
  • Expanding the initiative to support civic projects (like regional reservoir ballot initiatives)
